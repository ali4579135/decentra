Introducing

DecentraEatz Is A Transportation Platform Designed For Different Types Of Communities To Purchase And Get Fast Food At Their Doorstep With Ease And Convenience. As A Result Of The Most Recent Technological Revolution, The Globe Is Becoming Increasingly Digital, Where Every Occurrence Is Ultimately Swift. As A Result Of Our Belief In Modernization, Our Company Will Be Based In The UAE Dubai And Has Global Expansion Plans. We Offer Clients A Variety Of Learning, Earning, And Entertainment Options. We Are Launching A Mobile Application That Will Incorporate All Elements That Will Be Advantageous To Our Clients. It Will Contain A Dashboard For Ordering Fast Food From Which Users Can Place Their Orders.

We Have Our Own Crypto Currency, Into Which Users Could Easily Change Fiat Currency And Other Currencies. Our Clients And Users Have Access To Direct Crypto Payment Options, Allowing Them To Swap Their Cash Quickly And Complete The Transaction. In Addition, DecentraEatz Has Its Own Delivery Vehicles That Our Personnel Will Use To Ensure The Prompt And Secure Delivery Of Orders To Customers. We Do Not Obligate The Public To Merely Incur Expenses From Us; Rather, We Offer Employment Opportunities To Individuals, Who Will Bring Their Own Automobiles And Register As Our Employees. We Will Provide The Best Rewards To Our Service-Providing Workers And Drivers.

OUR MISSION

Our Objective Is To Provide A Platform With Diverse Food Delivery Options For Individuals. As A Result Of Our Desire To Simplify And Expedite Life, We Are Incorporating Crypto Experiences Into Our Services. We Want To Optimize Crypto Purchases With The Best Consumer Incentives. We Want To Enable Users To Pay For Fast Food With DecentraEatz, Which Are Best For Receiving Services Quickly. Our Objective Is To Create A Platform With A Modern Food Delivery System That Is Competitive.

OUR VISSION

The Mission Of DecentraEatz Is To Provide Its Users With A Platform That Is Reliable, User-Friendly, And Lightning Quickly So That They Can Meet All Of Their Requirements In A One Place. Our Goal Is To Expand Our Business To The Fullest Extent Possible Across The Globe So That We Can Meet The Needs Of As Many Different Kinds Of Communities As Possible. We Want To Simplify The Conversion Of Local Payments To EATZ Within The Mobile App As Quick As Possible So That Customers Can Pay With EATZ To Place Their Orders.

OUR ECOSYSTEM

DecentraEatz Is Developing Its Own Ecosystem To Increase Customer Loyalty, Which Will Have A Positive Effect On Its Revenue. Our Ecosystem Will Be More Robust And Appealing To Users. We Include Driving Employment, The Quickest Crypto Transactions, And Dependable Manufacturing And Delivery Technology. Our Cryptocurrency Token Operates On The Ethereum Network, Which Allows Users To Trade DecentraEatz Tokens. Our Token Is Quicker Than The Competition. We Want To Provide The Highest Level Of Customer Satisfaction Possible, And One Of The Ways We Do This Is By Accepting DecentraEatz Payments For The Delivery Of Quick Food.

The majority of consumer rewards programmes are geared for long-term participation.
